Bio

Andrew has always had a knack for numbers and an interest in exploring what meaning they have. From playing math based video games as a young child to competing in math competitions in middle school to coaching math teams and eventually getting a degree in Applied Math, he has discovered that Math is an endless field of study that can truly be used to gain insight. He has extensive leadership experience as he spent many years as a Math Coach and a Youth Baseball Coach. He graduated from The University of Wisconsin with an Applied Math B.S. (emphasis in Computer Science) and as well as a Mandarin Chinese Language B.A. Andrew currently studies Analytics as a Master's student at Northeastern University and actively seeks out opportunities to apply his data science skills.
